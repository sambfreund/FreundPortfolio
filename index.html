Who am I?

Senior Quality Engineer with 9+ years of experience. I've implemented automation and AI-driven testing processes to greatly reduce time investments in QA and improve cross-team efficiency. My early background in Software Development gives me a unique perspective when collaborating with development teams.

I'm passionate about communication and always consider multiple perspectives across teams. From my bachelor's degree in game design to building school information systems as a developer, and now leading QA efforts at Don't Panic Labs—I've consistently focused on delivering quality and fostering collaboration.

On a personal note, I'm a husband, father (to two humans, a cat, and a dog), life-long learner, and nature-lover who enjoys disc golf, writing, photography, gaming, sports, movies, music, books, bird watching, and plenty more. Variety really is the key spice in my life.
