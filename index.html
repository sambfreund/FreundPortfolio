Who am I?

Hi, I'm Sam. I have a large array of interests that has repelled boredom my entire life. Some of my hobbies led to getting a bachelor's degree in game design, which in turn taught me the basics of programming, working within teams, and developing plans of execution.

These skills helped me get my first job as a PHP Developer. After a few years as a developer, I transitioned to QA. Now, today I am with a new company continuing my career as a quality assurance analyst. Working in QA has suited me well for various reasons. It allows me to be a part of product planning and sharing my vision for a prodct, occasionally sifting through code to help solve problems, and more recently I've been diving into automated testing which scratches my coding itch.

On a more personal note, I am a husband to my beautiful wife, new father to my smiley daugther, and friend to my furry dependants. I have been getting lost in my hobbies of disc golf, writing, photography, gaming, sports, movies, music, books, bird watching, and plenty more. My passion for learning new things is a fault in it's own sense. Variety really is a key spice in my life.

Thank you for taking the time to read this, and I'd love to hear from you. Feel free to reach out to me via my links below.
