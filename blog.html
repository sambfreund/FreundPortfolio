Nearly every day, I attempt to pull together a decent collection of relevant news. Most of it is nerdy news, and I try to stay away from politics. Thanks for being here!

A bit of a ramble... So I've been working on waking up earlier again. Getting back to my old ways. This has been helpful in terms of getting things done in the morning, but an issue I've found with this is that if you don't sleep well... you're super tired., and getting a few extra hours of sleep may have done more good than harm. However, I've been slacking on my exercise lately so that may be a factor. This made me think, "can waking up early make it easier to measure all of the other balances in your life?" ex: nutrition, exercise, mental heath, etc. Being an early riser isn't going to make you super self-conscious or anything, but I do think those factors might be smidge easier to pick out. What do you think?
